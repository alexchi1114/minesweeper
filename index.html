Directions



  • (1) Click on any box to get started
  • (2) If you click on a mine...you lose!
  • (3) The numbers in the boxes tell you how many mines are adjacent (are touching) the given box. This counts mines that are diagonal to a given box.
  • (4) You can right click on a box to 'flag' a box. This is useful for keeping track of which boxes you have determined are mines. You only get as many flags as there are mines.
  • (5) Once you have clicked all of the boxes that aren't mines, you win!
